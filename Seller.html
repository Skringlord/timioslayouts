What goes into a sale?
Selling a home requires lots of planning, preparation, and execution. Here are some of the most important factors in making sure you’re prepared for a smooth and painless transaction.
Determining the value of your home. There are many ways to determine how much your home is worth, but not all methods are completely accurate. To get the most precise value available, contact Timios. We will send you a free comprehensive property profile report using the most accurate, up-to-date data and neighborhood comparisons. No strings attached.
Staging your home. There are lots of tips out there, but the number one thing to keep in mind is eliminating clutter from your home. The biggest contributor to clutter in the home is almost always excess furniture. We recommend hiring a professional stager to help you decide what to keep and what to remove to make your house appear bigger to potential buyers. Also, removing clutter makes it easier for the buyer to imagine his or her items in the home.
Determine your costs. This includes determining the payoff amount for your current mortgage and/or any other items related to your home. Also, it helps to plan for closing costs, moving costs, and other expenses. Our closing cost calculator gives you an accurate figure for standard items, and your dedicated Timios representative can help with figuring any additional costs.
